Bo Zhang is now a second-year Ph.D. student at the School of Computer Science, Northwestern Polytechnical University, supervised by Prof. Helei CUI. His research interests include privacy computing and system security. He also worked on decentralized cloud storage and encrypted data deduplication. He has published papers in top-tier conferences such as USENIX Security and IEEE INFOCOM and flagship transactions such as IEEE TDSC and JISA.

During his undergraduate studies, he was among the inaugural cohort of the Kang Jichang Class. He graduated in 2023 as an Outstanding Graduate (ranking 1/227) of the School of Computer Science at Northwestern Polytechnical University.
